How to Use the Image Converter

  1. Click "Choose Image" or drag & drop an image into the upload area
  2. Select the output format you want to convert to (JPG, PNG, WEBP, etc.)
  3. Adjust the quality slider if needed (higher quality = larger file)
  4. Click "Convert Image" to process the conversion
  5. Download the converted image using the "Download Converted" button

Note: All conversions happen in your browser. Your images are not uploaded to any server.
