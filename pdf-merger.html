How many PDFs can I merge at once?

You can merge unlimited PDF files at once. The only limitation is the total file size, which must not exceed 150MB. Most users merge 5-20 files at a time without issues.

Can I rearrange the order of PDFs?

‚úÖ Yes! You can easily rearrange files by dragging and dropping them in the file list. The merged PDF will follow exactly the order you specify. You can also use the up/down arrows for precise control.

Are my merged PDFs secure?

üîí Maximum security guaranteed. Your PDF files are processed on secure servers and automatically deleted after merging (within 1 hour). We don't store your documents or share them with third parties.

What is the maximum total file size?

The maximum total size for all uploaded PDFs is 150MB. This is sufficient for merging dozens of typical PDF documents. If you have larger files, consider compressing them first using our PDF Compressor tool.

Can I add page numbers to the merged PDF?

‚úÖ Yes! Enable the "Add page numbers" option in the merge settings. Page numbers will be added to the bottom of each page in the merged document, making it easy to reference specific pages.

Yes, existing bookmarks from source PDFs are preserved. You can also enable "Add bookmarks for each file" to create new bookmarks for each merged document, making navigation easier in the final PDF.
